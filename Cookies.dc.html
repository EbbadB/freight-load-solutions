Cookie Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Freight Load Solutions LLC ("FLS," "we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website, and the choices available to you.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site function properly, remember your preferences, and understand how visitors use the site.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential cookies — required for the website to function correctly, such as remembering form inputs as you navigate the site.
  • Performance & analytics cookies — help us understand how visitors interact with our website (pages visited, time on site) so we can improve it.
  • Functionality cookies — remember choices you make on the site to provide a more personalized experience.

We do not currently use cookies for third-party advertising or ad retargeting.

3. Managing Cookies

Most web browsers let you control cookies through their settings, including blocking or deleting them. Note that disabling essential cookies may affect how parts of our website function. You can find instructions for managing cookies in the help section of your browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge, etc.).

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the technology we use or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
