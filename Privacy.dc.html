Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Freight Load Solutions LLC ("FLS," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Contact details (name, company, email address, phone number) submitted through our contact forms, quote requests, or carrier onboarding forms.
  • Shipment and freight details you provide when requesting a rate or booking a load.
  • Carrier information, including MC/DOT numbers, insurance documentation, and operating authority, submitted for carrier vetting.
  • Communications you send us by phone, email, or web form.

We also automatically collect limited technical information when you visit our website, such as browser type, device information, and pages visited, typically through cookies and similar technologies (see our Cookie Policy).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide freight brokerage services, including quoting, booking, and tracking shipments.
  • Vet and onboard carriers and agents into our network.
  • Respond to inquiries and provide customer support.
  • Send service-related communications and, where you've opted in, marketing updates.
  • Comply with legal, regulatory, and safety obligations applicable to licensed freight brokers.
  • Improve our website and services.

3. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Carriers, shippers, and agents as necessary to coordinate and execute a shipment.
  • Service providers who support our operations (e.g., form and email delivery, hosting, payment processing), under obligations to protect your data.
  • Regulatory or governmental authorities where required by law, such as FMCSA reporting obligations.
  • A successor entity in the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

4. Data Security

We maintain reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal and regulatory recordkeeping requirements (including FMCSA and DOT requirements applicable to freight brokers), and resolve disputes.

6. Your Choices

You may opt out of marketing emails at any time using the unsubscribe link included in those messages. You may also contact us directly to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, subject to our legal recordkeeping obligations.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are intended for business use and are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
