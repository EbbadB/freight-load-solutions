Our Story

Why We Started FLS.

Giao, P.J., and Farhan built FLS after years watching shippers get ghosted and loads fall apart because nobody was accountable. The problem wasn't technology — it was people who didn't pick up the phone.

We're not trying to be the biggest broker in America. We're trying to be the most reliable one for the shippers, carriers, and agents who work with us.

2021 — Founded
FLS opens in Pittsburgh, PA
MC authority obtained. First shipper accounts onboarded. Three founders, one mission: accountability on every load.
2022 — Growth
Carrier network scales to 48 states
Nationwide dry van coverage established. First 100 shipper accounts onboarded. Carrier vetting process built from the ground up.
2023 — Expansion
Agent partnership program launches
Independent freight agents begin plugging into the FLS network. 1,000+ weekly loads milestone reached.
2024 & Beyond
Expanding capacity. Same team, same standard.
Growing lanes, growing the network — without growing the distance between us and the people we serve.
